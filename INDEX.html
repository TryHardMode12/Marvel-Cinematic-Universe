The Marvel Cinematic Universe, commonly known as the MCU, is a universe depicting live action Marvel heroes fighting Marvel villains in order to save the world. Backed up by a wonderful cast and incredible writing, the MCU has been critically acclaimed.

The MCU is a great franchise due to its humor, action, acting, writing, directing, and shared universe. The shared universe has allowed characters to join together and set their differences aside.
